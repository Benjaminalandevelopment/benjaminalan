Project Description

This project was developed for an Architecture client who wanted a blog style website to display their current and on-going projects. Our team worked directly with the client to plan, design, and develop the project from the ground up over a 10 week period. The project was worked on by a team of 7 individuals and my role in the project was the team lead.
